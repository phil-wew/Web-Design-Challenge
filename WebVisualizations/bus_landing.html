Summary

This project utilizes Census APIs, Texas A&M's reverse geocoding API, and data provided from Valley Metro in Phoenix, Arizona.

I began with a dataset from Valley Metro containing ridership averages and another containing a complete list of bus stops. The first task was to reverse geocode the bus stop locations in order to find their corresponding zipcodes; the zipcodes would later be necessary to use the Census API.

After some data wrangling, reverse geocoding the zipcodes, and making some calls to the Census API, I was then able to group my data by zipcodes and determine the population size, per capita income, and poverty levels. With I then took this data and used Matplotlib and Numpy to create visualizations and analyses to determine correlations between ridership numbers and the demographic trends pulled from the Census data. Lastly, I present my findings for two seperate categories - weekends and weekdays.
